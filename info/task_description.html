Hey, are you ready for some game party in Scrabble?
You have a list of words and you have to find only one - the most valuable of them.
Rules:
Each word have a worth that equivalent a sum of letters which it consists of.
The values of letters are:
e, a, i, o, n, r, t, l, s, u = 1
d, g = 2
b, c, m, p = 3
f, h, v, w, y = 4
k = 5
j, x = 8
q, z = 10
For example, the worth of 'dog' is 5, because 'd' = 2, 'o' = 1 and 'g' = 2.

Input: List of words

Output: The most valuable word

How it is used: For lexicographical analysis of a texts.

Precondition:
2 <= words <= 10
Real words only
lowercase letters only
